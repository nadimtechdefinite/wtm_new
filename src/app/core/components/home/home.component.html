FAQs
How I can register the grievance?
  1. The citizen shall visit the “Write to RD Minister” Portal.
  2. The citizen shall click on Citizen Registration and complete the registration form in all respects.
  3. After successful registration, the citizen shall navigate to the Login page.
  4. The citizen shall select the Citizen option and enter the registered mobile number.
  5. The citizen shall enter the One-Time Password (OTP) received on the registered mobile number and click on Verify.
  6. Upon successful login, the citizen shall click on Register Grievance, fill in the grievance details, and upload supporting documents, if any.
  7. The citizen shall submit the grievance by clicking on the Submit button.
  8. On successful submission, the grievance shall be registered in the system.
  9. The citizen may download the acknowledgement PDF for future reference and tracking of the grievance.
 How do I track my grievance status?
  1. The citizen shall visit the Write to RD MinisterPortal.
  2. The citizen shall navigate to theLoginpage.
  3. The citizen shall select the Citizen option and enter the registered mobile number.
  4. The citizen shall enter the One-Time Password (OTP) received on the registered mobile number and click on Verify.
  5. Upon successful login, the citizen shall select the option Track Grievance Status.
  6. The current status of the registered grievance shall be displayed on the screen.
  7. The citizen may view and download the grievance details for future reference, if required.
(Note-The accuracy of translation depends on the availability and quality of Bhashini language models for the selected language.) How do I change the language using Bhashini?
  1. Open the application/portal where Bhashini is integrated.
  2. Click on the Language Selection or Bhashini icon available on the screen.
  3. Choose your preferred language from the list of supported Indian languages.
  4. Once selected, the content will be automatically translated and displayed in the chosen language using Bhashini services.
  5. You can switch back or change to another language anytime by repeating the same steps.
If citizen is not satisfied with the grievance status / solution?
  1. In case the citizen is not satisfied with the grievance redressed provided, the citizen may log in to the Write to RD Minister Portal using the registered mobile number.
  2. After successful login, the citizen shall view the grievance details under Track Grievance Status.
  3. The citizen may select the option Not Satisfied / Reopen Grievance (as available on the portal).
  4. The citizen shall provide remarks or reasons for dissatisfaction and may upload supporting documents, if required.
  5. The grievance shall be reopened/escalated to the concerned authority for further examination and necessary action.
  6. The revised status of the grievance shall be communicated to the citizen through the portal and/or registered mobile number.
